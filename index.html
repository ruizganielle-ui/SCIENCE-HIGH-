Welcome, DuScians! Proudly serving as one of the 17 Regional Science High Schools in the Philippines.

Our Story

Ramon Teves Pastor Memorial - Dumaguete Science High School was founded on August 10, 1988 (Ordinance No. 81, Resolution No. 6). Originally a single-class high school at the Dumaguete City National High School campus, we later moved to our permanent site in Daro.

The school was elevated to a Regional Science High School on July 21, 1993, by virtue of DECS Order No. 69, s. 1993.

Student Life

We consist of six levels: Innovators, Seekers, Explorers, Analysts, Probers, and Experimenters. (Note: Researchers and Discoverers have been retired as of 2026).
